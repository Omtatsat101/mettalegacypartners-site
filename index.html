How I work

A small practice. The point is to protect the owner, get the valuation right, and bring the real estate along when it's part of the deal.

01

Confidential by default

Listings are blind, financials are gated, and conversations with staff, vendors, and customers stay protected through close.

02

Valuation grounded in cash flow

SDE / EBITDA recasts with documented adjustments. We tell sellers what the number is, not what they want to hear.

03

Real estate, when present, runs in parallel

If owned real estate is part of the deal, Metta Realty Partners handles the property side under one timeline and one client experience.

04

Buyer quality over buyer count

Vetted operators, family offices, and strategics. NDAs first, financials after, LOIs from people who can actually close.

Services

Sell-side, buy-side, and succession

A focused engagement at each stage. Real-estate brokerage is included when the deal involves property.

Sell-side advisory

2 to 10 month engagements

Confidential listing prep, recast financials, CIM and teaser, buyer search, NDA gating, LOI to close. Standard mainstreet through lower-middle-market mandates.

Buy-side search

retained engagement

Defined mandate, off-market sourcing, owner outreach, LOI through close. For operators, search funds, and family offices with a specific thesis.

Valuation & readiness

single deliverable

SDE / EBITDA recast, market-multiple comp set, written opinion of value, and a punch list to lift the number before a real sale process.

Operating + real estate combo

with Metta Realty Partners

Operating business brokered by Metta Legacy Partners. Real estate — fee-simple building, multi-tenant property, residential keeper — brokered by Metta Realty Partners under HomeSmart. One closing schedule.

Succession planning

12 to 36 month horizon

For owners who aren't selling yet but want to be ready when the market or family situation changes. Documentation, financial hygiene, and a clean cap table.

Exit acceleration

project basis

You already have an interested buyer or LOI. We professionalize the process from there — diligence room, deal structure, financing intros — and protect the seller through close.
